Stability fixes and a live throughput graph.

- The VPN notification now draws a small live graph of recent up and down speeds next to the existing rate readout.
- Apps you pick to route through the tunnel no longer reset when you switch the active profile.
- The connection status in the app stays in sync with the real tunnel. It no longer sticks on "Connected" after the background service stops, and reopening the app clears a stale status.
- Fixed a crash when expanding a routing entry that resolves to a large number of networks, such as a big ASN. The list now caps and shows a "+N more" line instead.
- A connection that cannot recover, such as one rejected for wrong credentials, now shows the reason on the dashboard and stops, rather than retrying for minutes.
- Importing a profile now accepts the connection JSON that Bedlam copies to the clipboard, not only a `hysteria2://` link, so you can paste a config copied from the app straight back in.
- Trimmed the routing presets and tidied parts of the interface.
- Updated the bundled Hysteria core to 2.9.3.
