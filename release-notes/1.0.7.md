New connection options from upstream Hysteria.

- Realm rendezvous mode: point the server address at a `realm://` or `realm+http://` URL and the client reaches a peer behind NAT through STUN and UDP hole punching, no public IP or port forwarding needed. STUN servers, timeouts, and the rendezvous-TLS insecure flag live in a Realm section that appears when the address is a realm URL.
- Gecko obfuscation: builds on Salamander and fragments the QUIC handshake into random-sized padded chunks. Select `gecko` as the obfuscation type; min and max packet size fields appear, defaulting to 512 and 1200.
- Realm connections re-run STUN and hole punching on reconnect, and their rendezvous traffic is kept off the VPN so reconnects work while the tunnel is up.
